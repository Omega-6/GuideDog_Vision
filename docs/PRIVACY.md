# Privacy Policy - GuideDog Vision

Last updated: April 6, 2026

## Data Collection
GuideDog Vision does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Camera
The app uses your camera to detect obstacles. All image processing runs on your device. Images are not saved or transmitted, except when you manually trigger a scan (double-tap), which may send a single image to our cloud AI service for scene analysis. These images are processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

## Microphone
The app uses your microphone for voice commands only. Audio is processed on-device and is not recorded or stored.

## LiDAR
LiDAR depth data is processed entirely on-device and is never transmitted.

## Third-Party Services
When you trigger a manual scan, images may be sent to Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI (ChatGPT) for scene description. These services process the image and return a text description. No images are retained by these services after processing.

## No Account Required
No sign-up, login, or personal information is required to use the app.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, visit https://github.com/Omega-6/GuideDog-Vision
